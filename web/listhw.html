  4. Anime Terminology
    5. Shonen :
    A genre of anime targeted at a young male audience, typically featuring action-packed storylines and young male protagonists.
    Mecha :
    A genre of anime that features robots or machines controlled by people, often in a futuristic setting.
    Isekai :
    A genre of anime where the protagonist is transported to, reborn, or trapped in a parallel universe or fantasy world.
